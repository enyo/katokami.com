Katokami

Do it yourself is the basic idea behind the KATOKAMI label. KATOKAMI papercraft kits are unique in their distinctive 'polygonal' look and the fun and easy assembly. Pre-cut and engraved sheets of paper reduce the time needed for putting a model together and help to capture the essence of paper crafting: the joy of creating something with your own hands.
