Christmas Star

This papercraft kit of a (almost) lifelike Christmas Star flower is the perfect way to get away from the bustling shopping streets and into the christmas spirit. Put on your bunny slippers, make yourself a big mug of tea, get some glue and switch off for 2 hours while crafting this beautiful paper plant.
